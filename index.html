Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

At AquaWise, we prioritize your data privacy. We collect:

  • Personal Info: Name and email for account management.
  • Agricultural Data: Crop types, soil moisture readings, and field location.
  • Device Data: IP address and browser type for security purposes.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used strictly to:

  • Calibrate the machine learning models for your specific field conditions.
  • Provide historical usage reports and water saving analytics.
  • Send critical alerts regarding extreme weather or system updates.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. We use trusted third-party services (Google Firebase) solely for data hosting and authentication.

4. Location Privacy

Precise location data is used exclusively to fetch hyper-local weather forecasts (rain, temperature, humidity) which are essential for the irrigation algorithm. This data is not tracked for any other purpose.
